Oil Spill Counter Widget

Embed the oil spill counter widget on your website to show your visitors how much oil is leaking from various sites!

See it in action:

Litres spilled at Cold Lake

How it works:

Simply include the following code snippet on your website to have an oil spill counter!

<div id="spillWidget"
        data-litres-per-day="186400"
        data-start-date="2013-01-01"
        data-initial-litres-spilled="0" ></div>
<script src="http://spill-widget.openwe.st/spill-counter.js"></script>

Note that you can change the values to change how fast the widget increments, based on the latest data for various oil spills.
